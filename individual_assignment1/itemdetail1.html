Call the Spirit Dragons

Tarkir: Dragonstorm

Rarity: Mythic

Casting Cost: W B Bk R G

Abilities: Dragons you control have indestructible.
At the beginning of your upkeep, for each color, put a +1/+1 counter on a Dragon you control of that color. If you put +1/+1 counters on five Dragons this way, you win the game.

Flavor Text: The essence of Tarkir was shaped into draconic embodiments of the re-formed clans.
