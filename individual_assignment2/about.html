Two Friends, One Goal: DNA of Music

We believe music is a conversation across generations. We started in a basement looking for a drum beat that was used on a classic hip-hop record. We then realized that music isn't a straight line, but more of a circle. We keep records for the crate diggers, producers, and fans.

The Vibe

Our bins are organized by popularity; they are organized by vibes. Whether you are a producer looking for that perfect beat or a jazz purist seeking a first pressing, you are home!
